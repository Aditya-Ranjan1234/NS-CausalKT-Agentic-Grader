ACE (Average Causal Effect)

Measures the intensity of the causal link. High ACE means mastering this concept "causes" success in the next one. Range: [-1, 1].

AUC (Area Under Curve)

Predictive precision. Higher AUC means the model is better at identifying if a student will get a specific question right.

RMSE (Root Mean Square Error)

The "Standard Gap" between prediction and reality. Lower values indicate more reliable, stable mastery estimates.
